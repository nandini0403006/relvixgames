About Relvix Games

Relvix Games was founded by Nandini K, a passionate game developer and B.Sc Data Science student. We specialize in creating engaging 2D games using Python and Pygame.

Our mission is to build games that are fun, accessible, and memorable. We believe in the power of games to bring people together.

Started in 2024, Relvix Games has published 2 complete games on itch.io. We are currently working on Word Search and planning more exciting projects.

Future plans include publishing on Google Play Store and expanding to mobile platforms.
